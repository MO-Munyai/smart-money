1. For USA Stocks : 
Use the plain ticker e.g NVDA

2. For RSA Stocks:
Use the .JO suffix , e.g CPI.JO

3. For UK Stocks:
Use the .L suffix , e.g BP.L

4. For Canadian Stocks:
Use the .TO suffix , e.g TD.TO 

5. For Australian  Stocks:
Use the .AX suffix , e.g BHP.AX

6. For Japan  Stocks:
Use the .T suffix , e.g 7203.T   (Toyota)

7. For German  Stocks:
Use the .DE suffix , e.g BMW.DE 

8. For France Stocks:
Use the .PA suffix , e.g MC.PA

9. For Netherlands  Stocks:
Use the .AS suffix , e.g ASML.AS 

10. For Indicies:
Use ^_ticker_
e.g ^IXIC

11. Crypto:
BTC-USD
ETH-USD
SOL-USD


ETFs follow the same naming convensions as stocks. 
Hit a quick Google search for the exact ticker you are looking for.
